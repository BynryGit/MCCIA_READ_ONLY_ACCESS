The committee was established with a purpose to create a synergy between the Defence establishments and the Industry.

The Committee functions with the following objectives;

  1. Assist industries in identifying products in defence supplies

  2. Guide Industries for identifying Defence requirements, Defence registration and other relevant matters.

  3. Create professional linkages amongst DRDO, Quality Assurance Agencies, Ordnance Factories, Defence Public Sector Units & the Industry.

  4. To create and nurture a think tank to generate ideas leading to stronger business relations.
