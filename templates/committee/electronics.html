The committee was formed with the purpose of giving boost to the Electronics Industry through an interaction and dialogue between the various stakeholders in the electronics eco-system.

The committee functions through the following initiatives

  1. Facilitate ideation and assist industries in identifying opportunities in Electronics and Allied Sectors.

  2. To organise relevant events and activities to serve as platforms for information and experience sharing.

  3. To create and nurture a think tank to generate ideas for the industry

  4. Encourage industry participation in the MCCIA Electronics Cluster Foundation (MECF)
