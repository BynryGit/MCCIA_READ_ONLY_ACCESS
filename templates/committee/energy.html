The energy committee was established to address the power issues of the industry and suggest a way forward. MCCIA has been making continuous efforts to improve the power situation in the Pune Region as well as the Maharashtra State.

The initiatives include;

  1. Improving Distribution Network

  2. Lobbying with Government for funding the Projects to drastically improve Power Infrastructure

  3. Efforts for additional power generation by sourcing from NTPC / Power Grid / Private Suppliers

  4. Suggestions for allowing unhindered generation of Captive Power

  5. Integration of Captive Power into MSEB grid to tide over peak hour shortage

  6. Efforts for maintaining power supply during Strike / potential strike by MSEB workers

  7. Representing for fair tariff for all classes of consumers and also wind power producers to Maharashtra Electricity Regulatory Commissions have yielded positive results over the last three years.

  8. Open Access to Electricity

  9. Single Point Supply to Commercial establishments consisting of different categories of consumers

MCCIA undertakes comprehensive Energy Audits for both Thermal and Electrical System.
