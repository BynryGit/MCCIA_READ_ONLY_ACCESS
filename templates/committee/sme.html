Vision:

 

MCCIA’s SME committee acts as a premier body to bring about a strong and enterprising SME sector in Pune district through –

 

A.      Facilitating and providing relevant, segment specific, implementable support to small and medium businesses towards overcoming adversity and exploiting good opportunities arising out of fluctuations / changes in market conditions and/or Govt. Policies.

 

B.      Acting as a catalytic agent in bringing out suitable changes in current Government policies and/or forming new policies towards supporting  the growth of SME segment.
