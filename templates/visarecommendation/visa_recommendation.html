Feel Free to Use Online Visa Recommendation form below to reach Us

Mahratta Chamber of Commerce,Industries and Agriculture has been issuing Visa Recommendation Letters for the people working for companies based in Pune region.
These letters facilitate overseas business travel to many countries. This Service is available only to our Members. Based on an Indemnity Bond (one time) executed by the Company, we can issue the Visa Recommendation Letters from our offices on Tilak Road / Senapati Bapat Road/Bhosari.
These letters are normally addressed to the Embassy or High Commission in New Delhi or Consulate /Deputy High Commission in Mumbai.

Charges for issue of Visa Recommendation Letters are Rs. 150 per letter.
