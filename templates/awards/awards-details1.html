ROTARY CLUB OF POONA WEST (RCPW) AND MAHRATTA CHAMBER OF COMMERCE INDUSTRIES AND AGRICULTURE (MCCIA) INVITES ENTRIES FOR BEST HUMAN RESPONSE AWARD FOR THE YEAR 2016-17

Rotary Club of Poona West and Mahratta Chamber of Commerce, Industries and Agriculture, Pune have instituted the “Best Human Response Award” for medium and large scale manufacturing & service industries in and around Pune region from the year 2009-10.

The objective of this award is to encourage and promote better Employer and Employee relations where the parties endeavor to arrive at solutions with apparently conflicting objectives and values like profit motive and social gain; discipline and freedom, authority and industrial democracy; bargaining and co-operation; coupled with conflicting interests of the individuals, groups and the community involved.

We firmly believe that this prestigious award will promote high ethical Employer –Employee Relations practices, which consequently would generate conducive employee response in the industries not only in Pune region but all over Maharashtra state. We also believe this will promote healthy industrial growth and prosperity to its constituents.
