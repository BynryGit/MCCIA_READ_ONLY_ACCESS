Psychometric test to assess Entrepreneurship Potential (BEAM):

MCCIA and Jnana Prabhobini have launched the joint Initiative for Assessing Entrepreneurship Potential of an individual. During the last couple of years, Jnana Prabhodini Shikshan Sanstha ( JPSS) has been working on this test. This battery of tests comprises a couple of sub-tests which are administered over a period of 3 hours with small breaks in between. No preparation is required by the candidates to appear for the test. At present, BEAM is a paper and pencil test, available both in English and Marathi.


Execution:

The test is administered by trained faculty from ATCG â€“ JPSS, following the standard procedures of psychological test administration. Each group is monitored by two psychologists. After the test, a report will be given to the individual in about 10 working days. This is followed by Individual Consulting Sessions. Full confidentiality will be maintained.


Eligibility:

Age: 18+ years
Education: 12 th + (Any stream)
Medium of instruction: English/Marathi.
Number of candidates: Preferably in Batches of 10 to 30 candidates at a time.


Venue: MCCIA, Tilak Road


Fees: Rs. 4000 inclusive of GST per candidate
