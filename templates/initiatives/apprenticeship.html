The Model:

Under the said guidelines of NAPS, the Apprenticeship Training comprises of

  • Basic Training and
  • On-the-Job Training/Practical Training at workplace in the industry.

The basic training will be imparted at MCCIA whereas on the job training will be imparted in Manufacturing Companies from amongst the MCCIA Members and other industries in the Pune Region.

The MCCIA and Aspire Apprenticeship Facilitation Centre will provide the following facilities to the industry and students registered as apprentice.

  • Making infrastructure available for setting up Apprenticeship Facilitation Centre.
  • Promoting Apprenticeship training amongst its members including SMEs.
  • Appropriate trade identification based on industry needs.
  • Execute tripartite apprentice contract with the Government, the Industry and the Apprentices.
  • Appropriate Trade Identification based on Industry Needs.
